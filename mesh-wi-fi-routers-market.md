<p><p>Mesh Wi-Fi routers come in various market types, primarily categorized as bi-band, tri-band, and quad-band. Bi-band routers operate on two frequency bands (2.4 GHz and 5 GHz), providing decent coverage for basic needs. Tri-band routers add an additional 5 GHz band, enhancing speed and performance for multiple devices. Quad-band routers, the latest innovation, include two 5 GHz bands and one 2.4 GHz band, offering superior bandwidth and minimizing congestion for high-demand environments, ideal for extensive smart home setups.</p></p>
