<p><p>The wound closure and advanced wound care market encompasses two primary types: primary and secondary wound closure products. Primary wound closure products, such as sutures, staples, and adhesives, are designed for immediate closure of surgical incisions or traumatic injuries, promoting optimal healing with minimal scarring. In contrast, secondary wound closure products, including dressings, special gels, and biologics, are utilized for wounds that require additional healing time and support, often addressing chronic or complex wounds to enhance recovery and prevent infection.</p></p>
