<p><p>Chromatography media refers to the stationary phases used in separation techniques, crucial for various analytical applications. Silica-based media, known for their surface chemistry and high performance, are widely used in normal-phase chromatography. Polymeric RPC (Reversed Phase Chromatography) media offer greater stability and reduced adsorption, making them suitable for complex samples and harsh conditions. Other types include materials like agarose and cellulose, which cater to specialized applications in biochemistry and pharmaceuticals, enhancing versatility in separation methods across industries.</p></p>
