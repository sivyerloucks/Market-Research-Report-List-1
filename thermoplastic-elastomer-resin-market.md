<p><p>The Thermoplastic Elastomer (TPE) resin market comprises several key types. Styrene-based TPE (SBCs) offers flexibility and processability, making it popular in consumer goods. TPO/TPV combines thermoplastics with rubber, delivering enhanced durability for automotive and industrial uses. TPU is known for its high-performance characteristics such as abrasion resistance and is used in medical and sporting goods. Polyether ester TPE (TPEE) is valued for its elasticity and chemical resistance, while "Others" includes niche applications and emerging formulations.</p></p>
