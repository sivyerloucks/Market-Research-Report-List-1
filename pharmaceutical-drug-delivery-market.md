<p><p>The pharmaceutical drug delivery market encompasses various methods, each tailored to optimize therapeutic effects. Oral delivery is the most common route, utilizing tablets or capsules for systemic effects. Pulmonary delivery targets lung conditions through inhalers or nebulizers. Injectable delivery ensures rapid absorption via syringes or auto-injectors, suitable for biologics and vaccines. Topical delivery involves creams and patches applied directly to the skin for localized treatment. Each method addresses patient needs and drug properties for effective therapy.</p></p>
