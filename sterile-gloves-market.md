<p><p>The sterile gloves market consists of primarily two types: latex sterile gloves and nitrile sterile gloves. Latex sterile gloves offer excellent dexterity and tactile sensitivity, making them popular in medical settings but are associated with allergies for some individuals. Nitrile sterile gloves, made from synthetic rubber, provide a strong barrier, are puncture-resistant, and are an ideal alternative for those with latex sensitivities. Both types cater to various healthcare needs, emphasizing infection control and safety in clinical environments.</p></p>
