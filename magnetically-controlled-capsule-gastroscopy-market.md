<p><p>The Magnetically Controlled Capsule Gastroscopy market consists of three primary types based on the magnetic technology used. Electromagnets utilize electrical current to generate magnetic fields, allowing for precise control of the capsule's movement during gastrointestinal examinations. Permanent magnets provide consistent magnetic fields without the need for power, offering a more straightforward solution for capsule navigation. Magnetic Resonance technology enhances imaging and navigation capabilities, enabling detailed visualization of the gastrointestinal tract while maintaining minimal invasiveness in diagnostic procedures.</p></p>
