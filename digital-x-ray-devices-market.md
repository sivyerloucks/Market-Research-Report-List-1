<p><p>The digital X-ray devices market is primarily divided into two types: retrofit X-ray systems and new digital X-ray systems. Retrofit X-ray systems involve upgrading existing analog machines with digital technology, enhancing image quality and efficiency without substantial investment. In contrast, new digital X-ray systems are entirely modern machines designed from the ground up with advanced digital features, offering superior imaging capabilities, faster processing, and improved patient comfort. Both types cater to varying needs within healthcare facilities, optimizing diagnostic workflows.</p></p>
