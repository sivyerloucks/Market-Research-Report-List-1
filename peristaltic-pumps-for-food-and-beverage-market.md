<p><p>Peristaltic pumps for the food and beverage market are designed to move fluids without contamination. Roller peristaltic pumps utilize rollers that compress a flexible tube, creating a vacuum that draws fluid through, ideal for handling viscous liquids. Shoe-type peristaltic pumps employ a shoe mechanism to achieve a similar effect, offering gentle handling of sensitive products. Both types ensure precise flow control, are easy to clean, and minimize the risk of product degradation, making them suitable for various food processing applications.</p></p>
