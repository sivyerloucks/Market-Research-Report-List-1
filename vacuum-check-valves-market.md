<p><strong>The Vacuum Check Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lift Check Valve</li><li>Swing Check Valve</li><li>Butterfly Check Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum check valves market includes various types designed to prevent backflow in vacuum systems. Lift check valves use a disc that lifts off its seat, allowing flow in one direction and closing as pressure drops. Swing check valves feature a hinged disc that swings open with forward flow and closes on reversal. Butterfly check valves utilize a rotating disc to regulate flow, offering quick operation and compact design. Each type addresses specific applications in industrial processes, enhancing system efficiency.</p></p>
