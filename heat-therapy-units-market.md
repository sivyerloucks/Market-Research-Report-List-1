<p><p>The heat therapy units market consists of various types designed for therapeutic heating. Ultrasound diathermy units use high-frequency sound waves to penetrate tissues, promoting healing. Microwave diathermy units utilize microwaves for deep tissue warming, effective in treating chronic pain. Shortwave diathermy units employ electromagnetic waves to generate heat in tissues for pain relief and inflammation reduction. Infrared diathermy units use infrared radiation for surface tissue warming. Other market types may include advanced modalities and hybrid technologies for enhanced therapeutic benefits.</p></p>
