<p><strong>The Elastic Compression Bandages Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dynamic</li><li>Static</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elastic compression bandages market is divided into two main types: dynamic and static. Dynamic compression bandages provide varying levels of pressure, promoting blood flow and facilitating movement, making them ideal for active rehabilitation. Static compression bandages, on the other hand, deliver continuous, uniform pressure to immobilize injuries and reduce swelling, suitable for post-operative care or chronic conditions. Both types cater to different patient needs and are essential in managing various musculoskeletal and vascular issues.</p></p>
