<p><p>The Brake Wear Indicator market includes two main types: Electrical Indicators and Audible Indicators. Electrical Indicators use sensors to monitor brake pad thickness, providing visual alerts to drivers when replacement is needed, enhancing safety and maintenance efficiency. Audible Indicators, on the other hand, utilize sound alerts, such as squealing noises, to signal brake wear. Both types serve essential roles in vehicle safety, helping drivers address brake issues promptly and ensuring optimal vehicle performance throughout its lifecycle.</p></p>
