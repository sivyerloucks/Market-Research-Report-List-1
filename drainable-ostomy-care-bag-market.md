<p><p>The drainable ostomy care bag market consists of three main types: the one-piece system, the two-piece system, and skin barriers. The one-piece system integrates the bag and the adhesive barrier, simplifying application and removal. The two-piece system separates the bag from the barrier, allowing for easy changes while maintaining skin protection. Skin barriers are essential components that protect the skin around the stoma, ensuring a secure seal and preventing leakage. Each type offers unique benefits catering to individual patient needs.</p></p>
