<p><p>The magnetic needle detector market is categorized into two primary types: scroll and non-rolling. Scroll detectors employ a rotating mechanism to scan the surface, providing precise detection over larger areas. They are often used in applications requiring thorough surveys. Non-rolling detectors, on the other hand, utilize a fixed sensor for direct detection. While more suited for smaller, targeted searches, they offer quick and efficient results in specific locations. Both types fulfill unique needs in various industries, enhancing safety and efficiency.</p></p>
