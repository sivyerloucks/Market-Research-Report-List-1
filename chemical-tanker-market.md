<p><p>The chemical tanker market comprises three main types based on deadweight tonnage (DWT). Inland chemical tankers (1,000-4,999 DWT) transport chemicals along rivers and canals, emphasizing regional distribution. Coastal chemical tankers (5,000-9,999 DWT) operate along coastlines, catering to short-sea trades. Deep-sea chemical tankers (10,000-50,000 DWT) facilitate international shipping of bulk chemicals, enabling long-haul transport across oceans. Each type plays a crucial role in the efficient movement of various chemical products globally.</p></p>
