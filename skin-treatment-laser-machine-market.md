<p><p>The skin treatment laser machine market is divided into two primary types: ablative lasers and non-ablative lasers. Ablative lasers remove outer layers of skin, promoting regeneration and improving texture, making them effective for deep wrinkles and scars. In contrast, non-ablative lasers penetrate the skin without damaging its surface, stimulating collagen production and offering subtle improvements with minimal recovery time. Both types cater to various skin concerns, driving demand in aesthetic dermatology and cosmetic procedures.</p></p>
