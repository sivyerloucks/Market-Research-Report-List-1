<p><strong>The Electric AC Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Synchronous</li><li>Induction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric AC motors market is primarily divided into two types: synchronous and induction motors. Synchronous motors operate at a constant speed synchronized with the AC supply frequency, making them suitable for precise applications. Induction motors, on the other hand, rely on electromagnetic induction to generate torque, making them robust and widely used in various industrial and commercial settings. Both types cater to different efficiency, performance, and cost requirements, influencing their applications in diverse sectors such as manufacturing, HVAC, and automotive.</p></p>
