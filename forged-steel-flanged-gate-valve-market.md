<p><p>The forged steel flanged gate valve market is segmented into three primary types: manual, pneumatic, and electric. Manual gate valves are operated through handwheels, offering straightforward control for low-pressure applications. Pneumatic gate valves utilize compressed air for automated operation, ideal for high-speed processes. Electric gate valves are powered by electric actuators, allowing for precise and remote control, often used in complex systems. Each type serves distinct operational needs, catering to various industries such as oil and gas, water treatment, and power generation.</p></p>
