<p><p>The Navigation Satellite System Technology market comprises two primary types: Satellite Constellation and Satellite-Based Augmentation Systems (SBAS). Satellite Constellation refers to a network of satellites that work together to provide precise positioning, navigation, and timing services globally. SBAS enhances the accuracy and reliability of satellite signals by providing corrections and improvements, particularly in areas where direct signal reception may be weak or obstructed. Together, these technologies play a crucial role in navigation and positioning applications across various industries.</p></p>
