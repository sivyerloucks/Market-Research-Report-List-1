Condensing turbines maximize efficiency by converting steam into water, allowing for additional energy recovery. Back pressure turbines, on the other hand, operate with steam exhaust at pressure, useful for combined heat and power applications. Other types may include extraction or mixed-use turbines, offering versatile solutions for industrial processes. Each type serves specific energy generation and efficiency needs in various applications.</p></p>
