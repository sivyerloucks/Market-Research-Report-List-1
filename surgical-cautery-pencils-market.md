<p><p>The surgical cautery pencils market is divided into two main types: hand control pencils and foot control pencils. Hand control pencils allow surgeons to activate the cautery function through a switch located on the pencil, offering precise control during procedures. In contrast, foot control pencils enable activation via a foot pedal, allowing surgeons to keep both hands free for operating tools. Both types enhance precision and efficiency in surgical procedures, catering to different surgeon preferences and operational needs.</p></p>
