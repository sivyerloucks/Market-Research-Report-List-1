<p><strong>The Soy Flour (DSF) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full-fat Soy Flour</li><li>Defatted Soy Flour</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soy flour market is divided into two main types: full-fat soy flour and defatted soy flour. Full-fat soy flour retains the oil content from soybeans, making it rich in protein and fat, ideal for enhancing flavor and nutritional value in various food products. In contrast, defatted soy flour has had most of its oil removed, resulting in lower fat content while maintaining high protein levels, making it suitable for health-conscious consumers and applications in baking and meat alternatives.</p></p>
