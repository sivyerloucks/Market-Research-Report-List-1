<p><p>The NOx reduction catalyst market can be segmented into two primary types: honeycomb type and flat type catalysts. Honeycomb type catalysts feature a structured, cellular design that maximizes surface area for chemical reactions, enhancing efficiency in reducing nitrogen oxides (NOx) from vehicle emissions. In contrast, flat type catalysts provide a simpler design with a larger surface area per unit volume, often used in specific industrial applications. Both types are essential for meeting regulatory standards and improving air quality.</p></p>
